How much does a Product Animation cost?

Depending on your wants and needs it can range anywhere from $100 to $10,000. I recommend sending an email with your request and I'll give you a quote within 24 hours.

How long will it take to receive my final product?

It depends on the length of the product. It can be anywhere from 24 hours to 14 days depending on the size of the project.

Do I get revisions?

You get one revision for free. 3D is very intensive. All revisions after that will charge an hourly rate.

How can I get started?

Either send an email or WhatsApp message and receive an answer within 24 hours.

I need the text to be in a different language than English. Is this possible?

Yes. You can either send me the text you need, or we'll use AI to translate it.
